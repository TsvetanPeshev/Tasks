Ivancho calls a number good if it is divisible by any number other than 0.
For example: - 13 is not good - is not divisible by 3 - 36 is good - is divisible by 3 and 6 - 102 is good - is divisible by 1 and 2 - 103 is not good - is not divisible by 3
Help Ivancho write a program that finds the number of all good numbers between given A and B (inclusive).
Sign in
From the first line, enter the numbers A and B.
Exit
List the number of good numbers in a single line.
Limits
1 <= A <= B <= 100000
